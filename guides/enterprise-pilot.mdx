Enterprise pilots are for teams that need more than a self-serve synthetic simulation.

Yomira helps construct an audience dataset from real customer, market, and social source material, then simulates reactions to concrete options before they reach the market.

## Best-fit decisions

- Launch messaging
- Product concepts
- New business ideas
- Pricing changes
- Public communications
- Enterprise sales materials
- Japan GTM / market-entry messaging
- Content and GEO campaigns

## Pilot shape

- Duration: 2 weeks
- Starting price: 300,000 JPY / $2,000
- Scope: one decision theme, multiple concrete options
- Inputs: customer notes, interviews, reviews, CRM context, social data, market source material, and company context
- Output: decision report, reaction distributions, raw voices, risks, next tests, and dataset notes

## When to choose enterprise

Choose enterprise when:

- the decision has real revenue or reputation risk
- the target audience cannot be described generically
- you need grounded source material, not only a described persona
- the result will be shown to a team, client, or executive
- you want to compare several concrete options before launch
