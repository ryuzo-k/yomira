What it does

A decision map, not another opinion.

01

Recover the context

The skill extracts your options, constraints, tensions, assets, hidden audience, and desired outcome from the conversation.

02

Build candidate paths

Instead of atomic categories, it creates coherent paths: what to foreground, what to hide, who it is for, and what behavior follows.

03

Prepare the next test

If a path depends on people, it names the artifact and audience you should test next. Simulation is optional and separate.

Example

From vague options to real paths.

Weak output

“You could focus on buyers, personal branding, or AI.”

Decision Foundation output

Business acquisition profile: foreground the buyer problem, proof, service outcome, and contact path. De-emphasize age, side projects, and abstract philosophy.

Full self / artist-founder profile: foreground the human mind work, taste, essays, prototypes, and unusual point of view. Accept that buyer clarity will be weaker.
