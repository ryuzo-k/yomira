What the skill does

It turns messy thinking into candidate paths.

01

Extract

Pull out the user's current options, ingredients, constraints, tensions, and disliked directions.

02

Target

If the goal is ambiguous, identify the possible decision targets before mapping the wrong thing.

03

Path

Bundle the ingredients into coherent paths with purpose, audience, foreground, and action norms.

04

Bridge

Mark which paths need human reaction, interviews, market tests, or future simulation.

Example output

The deliverable is paths, not loose categories.

Use cases

Built for decisions where missing options are expensive.

Use it before product decisions, positioning, pricing, launch planning, content strategy, profiles, offers, and other choices where one narrow AI answer can quietly cost you money.

01

“What should I build next?” becomes candidate business paths with model, audience, wedge, distribution, and learning loop.

02

“How should I launch this?” becomes launch paths: X-first, Reddit-first, DM-first, video-first, partner-first, or waitlist-first.

03

“What should my profile say?” becomes profile paths: business account, full self, artist-founder, AI-native operator, or single-purpose account.
