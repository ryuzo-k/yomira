What the skill does

It turns “give me the answer” into “show me the whole space.”

01

Frame

Clarify the decision, desired outcome, constraints, current options, hidden audience, and irreversibility.

02

Lens

Create useful lenses: categories, audiences, surfaces, constraints, motivations, or continuums.

03

Map

List options under each lens, so the answer is explorable instead of one flat pile of ideas.

04

Test

Define what evidence, question, or small experiment would make the decision clearer.

Use cases

Built for decisions where the missing options are expensive.

Use it for product direction, positioning, pricing, launch plans, content strategy, offers, career moves, and other intangible decisions where one narrow AI answer can quietly cost you money.

01

“What should I build next?” becomes a map of wedges, services, content loops, APIs, and no-build tests.

02

“How should I launch this?” becomes a map of public, private, partner-led, paid, manual, and waitlist paths.

03

“What am I missing?” becomes explicit axes, blind spots, rejected assumptions, and next evidence.
